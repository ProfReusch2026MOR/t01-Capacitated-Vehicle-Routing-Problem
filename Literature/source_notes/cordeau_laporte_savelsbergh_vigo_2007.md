# Cordeau, Laporte, Savelsbergh and Vigo (2007) — Vehicle Routing

## Full Reference

Cordeau, J.-F., Laporte, G., Savelsbergh, M. W. P., & Vigo, D. (2007). Vehicle Routing. In C. Barnhart & G. Laporte (Eds.), Transportation. Handbooks in Operations Research and Management Science, Vol. 14.

## Problem Class

Vehicle Routing Problem, Capacitated Vehicle Routing Problem, Vehicle Routing Problem with Time Windows.

## Main Idea

This source explains the classical Vehicle Routing Problem and several important variants. It describes the depot, customer nodes, vehicle capacity, customer demand, route construction, and common exact and heuristic solution approaches.

## Relevance for Our Project

This source is directly relevant because our model uses a depot, Lidl customer nodes, pallet demand, vehicle capacity, and route constraints. It also supports the idea that vehicle routing problems are central in distribution management.

## How We Use It

We use this source to justify the basic model structure of our project and to explain why CVRP and VRPTW are appropriate problem classes for our case.
