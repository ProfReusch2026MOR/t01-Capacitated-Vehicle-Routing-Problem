# Dantzig and Ramser (1959) — The Truck Dispatching Problem

## Full Reference

Dantzig, G. B., & Ramser, J. H. (1959). The Truck Dispatching Problem. Management Science, 6(1), 80–91.

## Problem Class

Truck Dispatching Problem / early Vehicle Routing Problem.

## Main Idea

This paper is one of the foundational works behind vehicle routing. It studies the routing of a fleet of delivery trucks from a terminal to several delivery points. Customer demand and vehicle capacity are considered, and the objective is to minimize total mileage.

## Relevance for Our Project

This source is relevant because our project also deals with deliveries from one depot to multiple customer locations. Like the original truck dispatching problem, our model includes customer demand, vehicle capacity, and distance minimization.

## How We Use It

We use this source to explain the historical origin of the Vehicle Routing Problem and to justify why our project belongs to the routing and distribution optimization problem family.
